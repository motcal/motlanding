What you'll do

  • Define and own the bring-up of all new optical systems.

Basic Qualifications

  • B.S. or M.S. in Electrical Engineering, Optical Engineering, Physics, or a related field.
  • Experience hands-on experience in optical system design, test, or validation.
  • Proficiency with optical test equipment including bit error rate testers (BERTs), optical spectrum analyzers (OSAs), optical power meters, and high-speed oscilloscopes.
  • Strong understanding of optical communication principles, including signal integrity, insertion loss, extinction ratio, and eye diagram analysis.
  • Experience designing or operating automated optical test setups for high-throughput environments.
  • Demonstrated ability to debug optical failures and drive root-cause analysis across cross-functional teams.
  • Willingness to work extended hours and weekends as needed.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Prior involvement in scaling optical validation processes from R&D to mass production.
